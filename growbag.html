Coco Peat Grow Bags

Planet Coco's grow bags are the perfect medium for cultivating cut flowers. Whether you're growing roses, gerberas, or other florals, these grow bags provide the ideal mix of coco peat and coco chips to ensure proper drainage and aeration. Designed to meet the specific needs of the cut flower industry, Planet Coco grow bags help in producing vibrant, long-lasting blooms.

100 x 20 x 20 cm grow bags are available in 30% coco pith & 70 % coco husk ratio or 70% coco pith & 30 % coco husk ratio.

Our grow bags have a natural pH of 5.5 to 6
Moisture Level Below 15
UV stabilized Bags (12 to 48 Months) for optimum lifespan
