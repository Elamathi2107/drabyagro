Coco Peat Block

Made from 100% natural coconut husk, these blocks expand up to 75 liters when hydrated, providing excellent moisture retention and aeration for healthy plant growth. Coco peat is ideal for a variety of gardening applications, from potting mixes and seed starting to hydroponics. It improves soil structure by enhancing water retention and drainage, making it a sustainable and eco-friendly choice for gardeners. Available in 5 kg blocks, our coco peat is easy to use and ideal for both indoor and outdoor plants. Place your order today and give your plants the foundation they deserve!
