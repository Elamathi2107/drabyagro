Coir Yarn

Coir yarn, spun from the fibers of coconut husk, is a versatile and eco-friendly product used in various industries. Known for its strength, durability, and resistance to saltwater, coir yarn is widely used in:

Agriculture: For tying plants and making support systems in horticulture and vineyards.

High Durability: Coir yarn is strong and resistant to wear, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications.

Handicrafts: A popular choice for weaving mats, ropes, and eco-friendly carpets, adding an authentic.

Biodegradable: Being entirely natural, coir yarn is environmentally friendly and decomposes over time.
