Coir Fiber

Coir fiber is the natural fiber extracted from the outer husk of coconuts. It is highly durable, water-resistant, and has a rough texture, which makes it ideal for a variety of applications.

The construction industry is exploring biodegradable materials for insulation, soundproofing, and erosion control.

Coir is naturally resistant to mold and decay, giving it a longer lifespan compared to other organic materials like peat moss.

Its pH-neutral properties make it an excellent base for various horticultural applications, enhancing nutrient uptake by plants.
